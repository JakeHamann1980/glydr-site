About

The Team Behind GLYDR

We believe gamers deserve more than ten fingers. GLYDR was born from the idea that your feet are an untapped competitive advantage.

Our Story

From Kickstarter to Your Desk

GLYDR started as a Kickstarter project with a simple question: why aren't gamers using their feet? Your hands are already maxed out managing keyboard, mouse, and controllers. Your feet are just sitting there.

We designed, prototyped, and now manufacture GLYDR right here in Texas. Every unit is assembled with care, tested rigorously, and built to perform under the most demanding gaming conditions.
